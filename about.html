About House of Sapphires

Original Indian Designer Wear ✨ Where quality meets luxury. Discover timeless elegance, crafted with care and designed to shine. We bring you curated pieces that reflect sophistication, beauty and class from our boutique in Rajshahi.

Founded in Rajshahi, Bangladesh, House of Sapphires brings together traditional craftsmanship and modern aesthetics. Each piece is lovingly crafted using premium fabrics, intricate embellishments and meticulous attention to detail. Whether you choose a pre‑pleated saree or an elegant evening dress, our creations are designed to make you feel confident and radiant.

Our Vision

We celebrate individuality through fashion. Our goal is to offer pieces that embody timeless elegance and impeccable craftsmanship while supporting ethical production and local artisans. When you shop with us, you become part of a community that values luxury, quality and authenticity.

Visit Our Boutique

You can find us at Plot 99, BSCIC Sopura, Rajshahi 6203, Bangladesh. Visit our boutique to explore our collections in person and receive personalised styling from our team.
