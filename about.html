About Us

Our Story

Founded in Rajshahi, Bangladesh, House of Sapphires was born from a passion for luxury and craftsmanship. We believe that clothing should not only dress you, but also tell a story. Our team of artisans meticulously crafts each piece, drawing inspiration from the vibrant culture of South Asia and the elegance of modern design.

From pre‑pleated sarees to bespoke evening gowns and curated accessories, every collection reflects our dedication to quality. We source premium fabrics and incorporate intricate hand‑embellishments to create garments that are truly unique. When you step into House of Sapphires, you enter a world where tradition meets innovation and where every customer is invited to shine.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate individuality through fashion. We aim to provide an unparalleled experience for our clients by offering pieces that embody timeless elegance and impeccable craftsmanship. We believe in ethical production, supporting local artisans, and creating garments that you will cherish for years to come.

Location

You can find us at Plot 99, BSCIC Sopura, Rajshahi 6203, Bangladesh. Visit our boutique to explore our collections in person and receive personalised styling from our team.
